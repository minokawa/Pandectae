#### [Malaki versus People](Cases/Malaki%20versus%20People.md),GR221075,2021

#### [Acharon versus People](Cases/Acharon%20versus%20People.md),GR224946,2021

#### [Pulido versus People](Cases/Pulido%20versus%20People.md),GR220149,2021
> - a void ab initio marriage is considered void from the beginning and produces no legal effects except those declared by law concerning the properties of the alleged spouses and its effect on the children born to void marriages.
> - judicial declaration of absolute nullity is not necessary to prove a void ab initio prior and subsequent marriage in a bigamy case.
> - subsequent judicial declaration of nullity of a void ab initio marriage can be a valid defense in a bigamy case.
> - subsequent declaration of nullity of the second marriage does not render the accused liable for bigamy, as it merely confirms the inexistence of the marriage.
